Life's a journey, enjoy the ride.

Collaborate

If you want to go fast, go alone, if you want to go far, go together. In the world of software development, teamwork isn't just a plus —it's a must. A willingness to help and also receive help is important to good collaboration.
Stop, collaborate and listen.

Experiment

To whip up an omelette, you've gotta crack a few eggs. The same goes for coding as well as life in general. Jumping into something new can feel like diving into the deep end, but also incredibly exciting. Coding allows for quick experimentation and a quick means of cleaning up those broken eggs, thanks source control.
Bon appétit.

Life long learning

The day you stop learning is the day you stop living. There's always something new on the horizon. Even the most knowledgeable people don't have all the answers. This is especially true in the world of programming. The field is so extensive and forever advancing. You need to be willing to keep learning with alacrity.
Old dogs can learn new tricks.

Problem Solving

Programming boils down to problems and how we go about accomplishing these problems. Some days, it's smooth sailing; other days, you'll feel like headbutting your desk. But that's the thrill of it! If you have the right frame of mind you'll never be bored as there will always be fresh problems for you to go about solving.
It's not possible. No, it's necessary.
